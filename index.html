About CCIS

The College Behind the Seminar

The College of Computing and Information Sciences (CCIS) at Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa, Manila is one of the country's leading public computing colleges — offering Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Information Technology, and Computer Engineering.

Established to bridge academic excellence and industry-ready skills, CCIS has produced thousands of graduates who lead technology teams across the Philippines and abroad.

Computing Trends is the flagship annual seminar of CCIS — a platform to expose students to cutting-edge technologies, connect them with industry practitioners, and spark conversations about the future of computing.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous BS CS, BS IT, and BS CpE programs with CHED recognition and industry-aligned curriculum.

Research & Innovation

Active research groups in AI, cybersecurity, software engineering, and data science.

Industry Partnership

Strong MOU ties with tech companies, government agencies, and international universities.
