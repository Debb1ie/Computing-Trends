About CCIS

The College Behind the Seminar

The College of Computing and Information Sciences (CCIS) at Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa, Manila is one of the country's leading public computing colleges — offering Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Information Technology, and Computer Engineering.

Established to bridge the gap between academic excellence and industry-ready skills, CCIS has produced thousands of graduates who now lead technology teams across the Philippines and abroad. The college is committed to research, innovation, and community engagement through programs like Computing Trends.

Computing Trends is the flagship annual seminar of CCIS — a platform designed to expose students to cutting-edge technologies, connect them with industry practitioners, and spark conversations about the future of computing.
